# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Project discussions may be informal, but collaboration must remain useful. Participants are expected to:

- discuss code and behavior instead of attacking people;
- provide reproducible information and accept technical questions in good faith;
- respect privacy and never publish another person's identity, credentials, private messages, or unrelated logs;
- avoid spam, threats, harassment, discrimination, and deliberately disruptive content;
- follow GitHub's Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policies.

Imageboard culture is not an excuse to bring targeted abuse or personal information into this repository.

## Scope

These rules apply to issues, pull requests, discussions, release comments, and other project-managed spaces. Content from external forums is governed by those services, but copied material may still be removed from this repository when it creates a legal, privacy, or safety problem.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Decisions should be proportional, documented when practical, and focused on keeping development possible.

Report conduct or privacy concerns privately to `dashchan_2@mail.ru`. Do not open a public issue containing sensitive information.
