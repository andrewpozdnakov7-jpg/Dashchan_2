* Images and videos are now saved in the background without a blocking dialog; completion is reported through a notification.
* Added a file-name conflict setting: save a renamed copy, replace, skip, or ask every time.
* With Passcode enabled, posts rejected for being sent too quickly are queued and retried automatically; the result is reported through a notification.
* Added copying images to the clipboard from the gallery.
* Added options to remove hidden posts completely and show the year in post dates.
* Forum settings are now organized by channel; experimental read-only 4chan support is bundled into the main APK and disabled by default.
* Fixed the Predictive Back gesture in referenced-post preview dialogs.
